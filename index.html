AI Toolkit

To support your learning throughout the course, you may access the following AI and data tools:

  1. Google Spreadsheets
    Students can access Google Spreadsheets at https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets for data organisation, analysis, and collaborative work.
  2. Google Gemini (Free Tier)
    Students may use Google Gemini at https://gemini.google.com under the free tier. This allows exploration of AI-assisted learning even before completing registration or after the OSALP programme ends.
  3. Vertex AI Search (Registered Students Only)
    Students who have completed registration can access the full platform at Vertex AI Search
  4. AI Prompt Guide (Google Gemini)
    Students can access our prepared prompt guide at AI Prompt Guide (PDF) to directly view, copy, and paste prompts into Google Gemini.
  5. R Programming Online Compiler
    For later stages of the course, students can use https://rdrr.io/snippets/ as a free and secure environment to write and run R code.

Capstone Project & Showcase

Students work in groups of 5 to conduct either (i) risk analysis of a real fund/portfolio or (ii) design innovative insurance/annuity products. Full support via Microsoft Copilot and Zoom mentoring sessions. Final showcase held on 17 October 2026 (Saturday), 13:00–17:00 (CUHK Shatin Campus, TBC).
